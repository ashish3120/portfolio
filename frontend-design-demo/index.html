Our Story

Born from
sleepless nights.

NOCTIS was founded on the belief that the most honest hours are those spent in darkness. Our fragrances are composed between midnight and dawn — when the world falls silent and only truth remains.

Each essence is a nocturne: a musical composition translated into scent. We source the rarest ingredients from shadow-grown gardens and forgotten apothecaries, blending tradition with an unapologetic modernity.
